Advanced Analysis on FAANG Stocks

Based on 2 years of historical data

Correlation Matrix

Shows how stocks move together (1.0 = perfect correlation)

Higher correlation = stocks move together. Lower correlation = better diversification.

Risk-Adjusted Returns (Sharpe Ratio)

Higher Sharpe = better return per unit of risk

Sharpe Ratio = (Annual Return) / (Annual Volatility)
Rule of Thumb: > 1.0 is good, > 2.0 is excellent
