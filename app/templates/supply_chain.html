What happened?

TIn January 2025, NVIDIA (NVDA) dropped sharply, about 17% in one day, after news that cheaper AI models from DeepSeek might reduce the need for its expensive chips. This also hurt Taiwan Semiconductor Manufacturing Company (TSM), since it makes NVIDIA’s chips, so less demand for NVDA means fewer orders for TSM. Tesla, Inc. (TSLA) is less directly connected, but it still moved down because it is often grouped with AI and tech stocks. This shows market correlation because one piece of news about AI affected multiple related stocks at the same time, meaning their prices moved together due to shared industry ties and investor sentiment.
